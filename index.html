Loan Control

Take charge of your money, and clear out your loan debt.

Loan Control is a personal finance application that helps you organize, manage, and pay off your student loan debts. It includes tools to help you
plan a strategy and analyze the results fit for your financial situation.

First, we'll help you organize your loans and assess your financial situation.

Organize your Loans

  • Input Loan Info Input your loan data manually, or import the PDF of your loan statements from nslds.edu.gov.
